Sung Jinwoo

Early Story

Sung Jinwoo is known as the weakest hunter in history but still risks his life to go into dungeons regardless to help pay for his mother's hospital bills, until on one raid where he gains the power to level up as a hunter, a skill others do not have. He uses this to become stronger and look out for his family.

    Current Abilities
  • Can level up.
  • Has access to an inventory system.
  • Has access to quests that froce encourage him to level up.

The end

Sung Jinwoo has become the shadow monarch himself and has set out to single handedly defeat the evils that have tresspassed on the world for far too long.

    Current Abilities
  1. Anything he kills can be risen as a shadow, made to follow his will and command.
  2. He can store these shadows away, if he so wishes.
  3. Can travel between his shadows
